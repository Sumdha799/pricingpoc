Price Calculation Breakdown

StableUsed for Premium-tier customers. Anchors price partly to cost, resisting market volatility.
Price = 0.7 × (Cost × 1.3) + 0.3 × Cost Price
Cost × 1.3 = cost-anchor (30% markup). Cost Price = margin-derived floor. Blend = 70% anchor + 30% floor.
CompetitiveUsed for Standard-tier customers. Price is a blend of Cost Price and Comp — positions you at the midpoint of your floor and the market rate.
Price = blend of Cost Price and Comp
High-priceUsed for Wholesale/Competitor-tier. Prices above the Cost Price to capture maximum margin from bulk buyers.
Price = Cost Price × 1.15
Adds a 15% premium on top of the margin-derived Cost Price. Justified by volume commitments.

Revenue & Margin — Tariff Impact
Revenue (Stacked Bars)
Pre-Tariff Revenue = List Price (T=0) × (1−Discount) × Qty
Revenue Gap = (Final Price AT − Final Price BT) × Qty
= extra revenue from adjusting price upward for tariff

Margin Lines
Margin Before Tariff = model run with T = 0
Margin After Tariff = model run with actual T
Target Margin = tier margin target

Margin Loss (Scatter Bubble Size)
Margin Loss = 1 − (Cost×(1+T)) ÷ Final Price (T=0)
= margin achieved if you sell at the before-tariff price but bear after-tariff costs
Larger bubble = more margin at risk if price is not adjusted
