Price Calculation Breakdown

StableUsed for Premium-tier customers. Anchors price partly to cost, resisting market volatility.
Price = 0.7 × (Cost × 1.3) + 0.3 × List Price
Cost × 1.3 = cost-anchor (30% markup). List Price = margin-derived floor. Blend = 70% anchor + 30% floor.
CompetitiveUsed for Standard-tier customers. Splits the difference between your List Price and the market rate.
Price = (List Price + comp) ÷ 2
comp = Competitor reference price = Cost × 1.25. Midpoint keeps you competitive without sacrificing margin.
High-priceUsed for Wholesale/Competitor-tier. Prices above the List Price to capture maximum margin from bulk buyers.
Price = List Price × 1.15
Adds a 15% premium on top of the margin-derived List Price. Justified by volume commitments.
